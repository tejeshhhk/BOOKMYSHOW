Show Description

"Only Murders in the Building" is a captivating comedy-mystery series that follows three strangers who live in the same apartment building in New York City. They bond over their shared love of true crime podcasts and find themselves solving a murder mystery within their own building. The show combines humor, suspense, and quirky characters for an engaging viewing experience. With a star-studded cast and clever writing, this show is a must-watch!
