Movie Description

"Kalki 2898 AD" is a futuristic science fiction movie set in the year 2898. The film follows the journey of Kalki, a protagonist who embarks on a mission to save humanity from a dystopian future. Packed with cutting-edge visual effects and an intense storyline, the movie takes the audience on a thrilling ride through a futuristic world where technology and humanity clash.
