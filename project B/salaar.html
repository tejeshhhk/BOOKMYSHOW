Movie Description

Salaar is an action-packed thriller directed by Prashanth Neel, starring Prabhas in the lead role. The film follows the journey of a man who must confront the dark underworld to protect his family. The movie is packed with intense action sequences and a gripping narrative that keeps the audience on the edge of their seat.
