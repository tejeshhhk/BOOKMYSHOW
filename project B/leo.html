Movie Description

"Leo" is a gripping action-thriller movie that follows the story of a man seeking justice for the wrongs done to him. Starring Vijay in the lead role, the film delves into themes of revenge, redemption, and human strength. With intense action sequences and emotional depth, "Leo" promises to keep the audience at the edge of their seats. Directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, "Leo" is a high-octane thriller with unexpected twists and turns.
